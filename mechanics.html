ULAM MO BA ITO!?

Ulam mo ba ito? is a game where you get to know more about Filipino food. In a world full of adobo, sinigang, and tinola (don't get me wrong, those are to DIE for as well), we're here to help you discover your next favorite ulam!

MECHANICS:

1. Once you go to the play page (via the side menu or the play button) where you go to...play. You get to choose which mode you want to play. The options are easy, average, and hard. There is 1 item to answer per mode.

2. In each item, there will be a picture of a certain Filipino food that you will have to guess. You are also provided a keyboard to input your answer. We'll be giving you 1 clue and it is the number of letters the food has.

3. To submit the answer, click the "Kain Na!" button

4. If you answer correctly, you will earn 5 points for easy, 10 points for average, and 15 points for hard. A total of 30 points. However, if you answer incorrectly, so sad wala ng food gugutom ka niyan huhu. Just kidding! But you will obviously garner no points for that item.

It's a pretty easy game to play. So if you're ready, let's play. Alam mo ba ito, ulam na ito?
