THE GAME

Have you ever wanted to sharpen your mind and tingle your senses with mouth-watering food while discovering more about our own culture and cuisine? Well this is the game for you and other people ready to take on a challenge of flavors and aroma. Ulam Mo Ba Ito? is a game that aims to spread and celebrate Filipino Culture and Filipino Cuisine. It also test its players about their knowledge on Filipino Food. You might know the appearance, taste, and smell, but do you know the name? Take on the challenge and travel the three different flavorful lands of LuzViMinda. We are waiting for you mandirigma ng lasa.
